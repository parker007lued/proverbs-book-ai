## Simple Guide for Non-Technical Users

### What is Terminal?
Terminal is a program on your Mac that lets you type commands to control your computer. Think of it like a text-based way to talk to your Mac. It's safe and we'll guide you through exactly what to type!

### Method 1: Spotlight Search (Easiest!)

1. **Press two keys together:**
   - Hold down **Command** (âŒ˜) key
   - Press **Space** bar
   - Keep both pressed for a moment, then let go

2. **A search box appears** at the top center of your screen

3. **Type:** `Terminal`
   - You'll see it appear as you type
   - Don't worry about capital letters

4. **Press Enter** or **click on "Terminal"**

5. **A window opens** - that's Terminal!

### Method 2: Using Finder

1. **Click Finder** icon in your Dock (bottom of screen)
   - Looks like a blue and white face

2. **Click "Applications"** in the left sidebar
   - If you don't see it, click "Go" in the menu bar, then "Applications"

3. **Open the "Utilities" folder**
   - Scroll down to find it, or type "Utilities" in the search box

4. **Double-click "Terminal"**
   - It has a black icon with a command prompt symbol

5. **Terminal window opens!**

---

### Method 3: Using Launchpad

1. **Open Launchpad:**
   - Press **F4** key, OR
   - Pinch with thumb and three fingers on trackpad, OR
   - Click Launchpad icon in Dock (rocket ship icon)

2. **Type:** `Terminal`
   - A search box appears at the top

3. **Click on Terminal** when it appears

4. **Terminal opens!**

### "I can't find Terminal"
- Make sure you're typing "Terminal" correctly
- Try Method 1 (Spotlight) - it's the easiest
- Check that you're on a Mac (Terminal is Mac-only)

### "Nothing happens when I press Command+Space"
- Make sure you're pressing both keys at the same time
- Try clicking the magnifying glass icon in the top right instead
- Restart your Mac if Spotlight isn't working

### "Terminal looks scary"
- Don't worry! It's just text
- We'll tell you exactly what to type
- You can't break anything by following our instructions
- You can always close Terminal if you need to
