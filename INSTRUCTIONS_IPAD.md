## Troubleshooting

### File won't open
- Make sure you're using Safari (not Chrome or other browsers)
- Try saving to Files app first, then opening

### "Add to Home Screen" not showing
- Make sure you're using Safari (required for this feature)
- Scroll down in the Share menu
- Try closing Safari and reopening the file

### App won't work offline
- Make sure you opened it at least once with internet
- The app needs to load once online, then works offline

### Can't find the app
- Look for the cross icon âœ on your home screen
- Swipe through all your home screen pages
- Use Spotlight search (swipe down from middle of screen)
