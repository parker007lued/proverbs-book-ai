## Creating Your First Book

1. **Click "Create Project"**
   - Enter your book title
   - Enter your name as author

2. **Generate a Chapter**
   - Select a chapter number (1-31)
   - Choose content type (Commentary, Summary, or Devotional)
   - Click "Generate Chapter"
   - Wait for the AI to write it (takes 30-60 seconds)

3. **Add Images** (Optional)
   - Click "Generate Image"
   - Describe what you want
   - Image is created and added to your chapter

4. **Assemble Your Book**
   - Click "Assemble Book" tab
   - Review your chapters
   - Click "Assemble and Export Book"
   - Choose PDF or EPUB format
   - Your book is ready!

## Troubleshooting

### "App can't be opened because it's from an unidentified developer"
1. Right-click the app
2. Select "Open"
3. Click "Open" in the security dialog
4. This only needs to be done once

### App won't start
1. Make sure you ran `install.sh` first
2. Try restarting your computer
3. Launch the app again

### AI models not working
- The app downloads models automatically on first use
- This can take 5-10 minutes depending on your internet
- Be patient, it only happens once!

## Need Help?

If something doesn't work:
1. Check that all dependencies installed correctly
2. Make sure you have internet connection (for model downloads)
3. Try restarting the app
4. Contact support if needed
